MTM6161 Design Foundations

Overview (current) Courses Professors Schedule

Whether you are interested in web design, user experience or user interface design, photography or videography, a strong grasp of the rules of design are a necessity. Students learn the fundamentals of graphic design; including the principles of layout, typography and colour theory. Students use a variety of industry-standard software such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign while following current best practices.

Example assignments include, creating a photo essay documenting a topic or event of the students choosing, as well as designing a short comic strip.

The class is excellent for students who are interested in careers in, UX/UI, web design, graphic design, photography and videography.
