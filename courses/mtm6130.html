MTM6130 Responsive Web Design

Overview (current) Courses Professors Schedule

The majority of content today is delivered though the medium of mobile phones. Students learn the importance of designing mobile first and realizing their designs through HTML, CSS and modern web standards. Current best practices in web design and coding, such as accessibility and search engine optimization are stressed.

Example assignments include building a static website verbatim from a wireframe and mockup, as well as creating a website of the students own design.

The class is excellent for students who are interested in careers in web design or development.
